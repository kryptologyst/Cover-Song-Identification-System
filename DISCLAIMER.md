# Privacy and Ethics Disclaimer

## IMPORTANT: Research and Educational Use Only

This cover song identification system is designed exclusively for **research and educational purposes**. It is **NOT intended for production use** or commercial applications.

## Privacy Protection

- **No Personal Data Storage**: This system does not store, process, or transmit personal identifiable information (PII)
- **Local Processing**: All audio data processing is performed locally on your device
- **No External Transmission**: Audio files are not sent to external servers or third parties
- **Anonymization**: Filenames and metadata can be anonymized to protect privacy
- **Temporary Files**: Any temporary files created during processing are automatically deleted

## Ethical Guidelines

### Permitted Uses

### Prohibited Uses
- **Voice Cloning**: Do not use this technology for unauthorized voice cloning or impersonation
- **Biometric Identification**: Do not use for production biometric identification systems
- **Commercial Applications**: Do not use in commercial products without proper licensing
- **Privacy Violations**: Do not process audio without proper consent
- **Copyright Infringement**: Do not use copyrighted material without permission

## Technical Limitations

- **Bias**: The system may exhibit biases based on training data
- **Robustness**: Performance may vary with different audio qualities and conditions
- **Scalability**: Not designed for large-scale production deployment
